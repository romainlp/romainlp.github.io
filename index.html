What it does

Everything your remote
should do.

01 🔊
Volume control
Rotary knob with drag gesture. Optional safe-mode cap to protect your ears and speakers.
02 ⚡
Power & mute
Instant on/off and mute with optimistic UI — no perceptible lag.
03 🎛️
Input selection
Switch between Aux, Phone, STB, PVR, CD, BD, AV and Sat from a clean picker.
04 ⚖️
Balance
Fine-tune left/right balance from −12 to +12 with a smooth slider.
05 💡
Display brightness
Set the front panel to off, dim, or full — ideal for late-night listening.
06 🎚️
DAC filter
Linear Phase Fast, Linear Phase Slow, or Minimum Phase Fast.
07 ⏱️
Auto-shutdown
Inactivity shutdown: disabled, 30 min, 1 h, 2 h or 4 h.
08 🎧
Headphone override
Keep speakers active when headphones are plugged in.
09 🔍
Auto-discovery
Scans your local network and finds Arcam amplifiers automatically.
10 📊
Device info
Live readout of firmware version, sample rate, and output temperature.
11 🔒
Processor mode
Fixed-gain processor mode per input for home theatre pass-through.
